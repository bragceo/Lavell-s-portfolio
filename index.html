About Me

Lavell is the Co-Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Brag House. He has 20+ years of management and corporate experience representing and counselling major corporations, and emerging growth companies. With Brag House, Lavell had the dream to create the first vertcially integrated social network for nonprofessional college gamers and their communities; where he could give nonprodessional gamers all of the perks and excitements reserved for professional esports. Lavell felt in order to do this he needed to learn how to build this digital home from the ground up. As such, he has developed these development skills to bring his vision to life.
